About Us

The Grill on Main is a place where you can enjoy a great meal and a great night with friends and family. Since our establishment in 2003, our mission has been to serve our small community of Coldwater, Ohio. for fundraisers, providing meals for high school athletic teams, or simply giving locals a friendly face, we do it for you! We are famous for our Tennessee Chicken, as well as having the most extensive seafood menu in Mercer County. Enjoy your next meal at The Grill on Main, where the food is so great, you'll scrape your plate!
